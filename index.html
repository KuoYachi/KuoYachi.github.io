Biography

YaChi Kuo is currently a graduate student in the School of Journalism and Communication, Nanjing University. She majored in Teaching Chinese as a Foreign Language in School of Overseas Education, Nanjing University. She is a graduate student with a cross-disciplinary background. During her postgraduate studies, she is going to focus on political communication, especially in the field of media and gender.
